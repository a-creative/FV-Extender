Why I made this game It was made as a gift for my nephew and niece at my niece's christening. Therefore pictures of both of them are in the game. Goal of the game To get the best time.
Get time bonuses by Collecting coins Finish your time by Driving your car around and finish all the instructions. Control your car: [ Left/Right arrow keys ] = Turn your car left and right.
[ Up/Down arrow keys ] = Throttle and reverse. [ Enter ] = Save your car if it turns over(gives a time penalty). [ Alt + F9 ] = Turn music.
